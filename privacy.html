ChilliBite Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2023

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Order Information: Delivery addresses, order history, preferences
  • Payment Data: Processed securely through our payment partners
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information when using our digital platforms

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Improve our products and services
  • Communicate with you about your orders
  • Send promotional offers (only with your consent)
  • Ensure food safety and quality

3. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Delivery partners to fulfill orders
  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Marketing platforms (only with your consent)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Secure payment processing
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Limited access to personal data

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or email.
