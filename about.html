Our Story

HOW IT ALL BEGAN

From Humble Stall to Botswana's Favorite What started as a small food stall is now Botswana's go-to for bold, fast flavors. Our secret? Henry's fiery chilli sauce—a family treasure—paired with quick, craveable meals. By 2018, demand exploded, leading to our first full restaurant. Today, with three Gaborone spots and Francistown growing, we stay true to our roots: homemade taste at unbeatable value. Every burger, crispy chicken piece, and ice-cold drink carries our passion—and Botswana's rich culinary spirit. We're not just a restaurant; we're flavor custodians, serving heritage in every bite.

The Founder

Henry Mothobi - Gaborone born, family-taught - started a revolution in 2015 with his grandmother's recipes and a simple stall. What began as one man's dream is now Botswana's favorite fast-food story. Our secret? Real family flavors served at lightning speed. That first fiery sauce that won over Main Mall still stars in every meal today. When demand exploded, we grew - but never changed how we cook: onions chopped fresh, spices measured by heart, every burger grilled with pride. From our Broadhurst beginnings to three Gaborone hotspots and growing in Francistown, one thing stays true: fast food should taste like home. Not just any home - your home. Priced so you never have to miss that flavor. This isn't just food. It's your childhood memories served in every bite, your late-night cravings answered, your quick lunch made special. Botswana's flavor legacy - kept alive, one satisfied customer at a time.

"We craft bold flavors fast - where every bite sparks joy and keeps you coming back."
